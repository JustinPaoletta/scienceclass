SCIENTIFIC METHOD


A series of processes that people can use to gather knowledge about the world around them.

EXPERIMENT


A Scientific Procedure To Determine Something.

HYPOTHESIS


A Guess as to what will Occur during the Experiment.

INDEPENDENT VARIABLE


The Factors in the Experiment that do not Depend on other Factors.

DEPENDENT VARIABLE


The Factors in the Experiment that Depend on the other Factors.

CONTROLLED VARIABLE


The Factors in the Experiment that always stay the same.

CHEMICAL REACTION


A process in which a substance changes into a different substance.

CHEMISTRY


The branch of science that deals with substances, how they interact and change.
