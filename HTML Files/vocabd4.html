INNER CORE


A ball of solid iron and nickel at the center of the earth.

OUTER CORE


A layer of molten iron and nickel surrounding the inner core.

MANTLE


Earth's largest layer, between the core and crust, made of magma and rock.

CRUST


The Outer Layer of the Earth.

CONTINENTS


Any of the 7 huge landmasses on the Earth's crust.

TECTONIC PLATES


Large slabs of rock that piece together the Earth's crust.

EARTHQUAKE


Violent shaking of the ground, can be caused by volcanoes, tectonic plates crashing, etc.

MINING


The process of obtaining coal and other resources from the Earth's crust.
