THE SCIENTIFIC METHOD

This is how REAL Science is done. With this method we are able to learn a lot about the world, and know that what we are learning is true!



  1. Ask a Question
  2. Gather Information and Observe
  3. Make a Hypothesis
  4. Experiment and Test Your Hypothesis
  5. Analyze Your Test Results
  6. Present a Conclusion
